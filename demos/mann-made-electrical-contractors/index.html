What we do

Services for Yarra Valley homes & sheds

Each job's its own thing — quoted properly, scheduled properly, finished properly. Here's the bulk of what comes through the diary.

Multi-aspect solar installs

We design and fit panel arrays across north, east and west roof faces so you're pulling power from sunup to sundown. Best for Yarra Valley homes where one roof face won't carry enough panels on its own.

Switchboard upgrades

We swap out tired old boards for safety-switched units that meet current Australian Standards and handle modern loads like inverters, heat pumps and EV chargers. Good move if your lights flicker when the kettle and toaster go on together.

Home rewires and extensions

We run new circuits through renovations, extensions and older Healesville cottages where the wiring's seen better days. You get tidy cable runs, proper certification and a board diagram you can actually read.

Lighting design and install

From downlights through to outdoor sensor lights for the driveway and shed, we plan the layout so the room actually feels right at night. We'll match the colour temperature across the house so it doesn't look like a servo in one room and a pub in the next.

Repeat-job electrical maintenance

For folks who've used us once and want us back for the next thing — power points, ceiling fans, fault finding, the lot. One of our regulars put it best: consistently excellent every time, with pricing that stays competitive.
