What Daniel does

Everyday electrical, done properly

Most jobs around the home come back to the same handful of things. Here's where Daniel spends his time across Mount Evelyn and the Yarra Ranges.

— 01 —

Downlight installation

Replacing tired old fittings or starting from scratch with a fresh layout across the whole house. Customers have specifically called out the neat finish on downlight jobs — no plaster dust left behind, no wonky spacing.

— 02 —

Switchboard upgrades

Swapping out old ceramic-fuse boards for a modern unit with proper safety switches on every circuit. Suits older Yarra Ranges homes that still have the original 70s board hanging on by a thread.

— 03 —

Power points & rewires

Adding extra GPOs where you actually need them, USB points, outdoor-rated outlets for the shed or alfresco. Also full or partial rewires when a renovation opens up the walls.

— 04 —

Safety inspections & repairs

Diagnosing the trip you can't figure out, the light that flickers, or the smell of something hot behind the wall. Honest assessment of what needs doing now versus what can wait.

— 05 —

General home electrical

Ceiling fans, exhaust fans, smoke alarms, oven and cooktop hookups, EV charger installs — the everyday stuff that keeps a house running. Quoted upfront over the phone where possible so you know what you're in for.
