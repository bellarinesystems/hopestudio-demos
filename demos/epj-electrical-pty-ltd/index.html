What we cover

Services, plain and simple

From a fresh rooftop solar array to a single dodgy powerpoint — same sparky, same standard.

01

Residential solar installs

Full rooftop solar systems sized for your home and your power bill, installed cleanly to Australian Standards. Same as Rob's install — handled start to finish with workmanship you'd be happy to point at.

02

Switchboard upgrades

Old ceramic fuses and tired boards swapped out for safety switches and modern circuit protection. Ideal for older Chirnside Park and Yarra Valley homes that were never wired for today's appliance load.

03

Powerpoints and rewiring

Extra GPOs in the kitchen, USB points in the bedrooms, or a full rewire during a reno. We tidy up dodgy older work and leave the walls in a state your painter won't curse us for.

04

Lighting design and install

LED downlights, pendants, outdoor and sensor lighting laid out properly so the room actually works at night. Good for renovators who want the lighting plan sorted before the plasterer turns up.

05

After-hours emergency callouts

Lost power, a tripping board, or a burning smell at the powerpoint — give us a ring any day, any hour. You shouldn't have to sit in the dark until Monday morning.
