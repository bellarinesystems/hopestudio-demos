What we sort

The full workshop menu.

01

Logbook servicing

We follow the manufacturer's schedule so your new-car warranty stays intact, and stamp the book properly. Suits anyone on a lease, novated arrangement or just wanting to keep resale value up.

02

Brakes, suspension & steering

Pads, discs, shocks, bushes and the whole front end — the bits that cop a flogging on West Tamar Highway potholes and gravel side roads. We'll let you know what needs doing now and what can safely wait.

03

Diagnostics & engine repairs

Warning light on the dash or a noise you can't quite place? We plug in, road test, and track down the actual cause rather than throwing parts at it.

04

Pre-purchase inspections

Thinking of buying a used car or ute? Bring it in and we'll give it a proper once-over so you know what you're really looking at before you hand over the cash.

05

Tyres, batteries & rego checks

The everyday bits that keep you legal and on the road — fitting, balancing, replacement batteries and roadworthy inspections. Quick turnaround so you're not stuck without wheels.

About the shop

The workshop down the road.

We're a small independent shop in Legana, and when you ring up or pull into the driveway, you're talking to the person who'll actually be under your bonnet. We handle the lot — logbook servicing, brakes, suspension, diagnostics, the curly stuff that pops up after a few years on Tassie back roads.

Most of our work comes from people up and down the Tamar corridor who've figured out it's easier to drop the car off five minutes from home than fight the traffic into town. If we spot something dodgy, we'll show you and explain it in plain language before we touch it.
