What we do

From a stuck car boot to a whole new lock system

01 / Residential

Residential lock installation

We'll come out, measure up your doors and have new deadlocks or entry sets fitted — often the day after you walk in, the way we did for David and his place. Suits homeowners who want the job done properly, not a flat-pack fix.

02 / Emergency

Emergency lockouts

Locked your keys in the car, the house or the shed? We turn up, work the problem and stay with it until you're back inside — even when roadside assistance has thrown in the towel.

03 / Key cutting

Key cutting and duplication

Bring the original into the shop and we'll cut spares for the house, the ute, the office or the gate while you wait. Restricted and high-security keys included.

04 / Commercial

Business and rural security

We fit and service locks, master key systems and padlocks for shops along Banna Ave, sheds out on the farm and everything in between. Built for operators who can't afford to be shut out at six in the morning.

05 / Safes

Safes and restricted keys

Whether it's a small home safe or a restricted key system for staff turnover, we'll talk through what actually fits your setup. No upselling, just what holds up.
