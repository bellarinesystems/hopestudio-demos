What we do

Locks, opened — fixed — upgraded.

01

Emergency lockouts

Locked out of your apartment, house or car at a hopeless hour? Ring Joe — past customers have had the door open within 15 minutes of calling, even on a Saturday night.

02

Lock changes & rekeys

New place, dodgy ex-tenant, or a key gone missing — we swap the lock or rekey the cylinder so your old keys are useless. We explain what we're putting in and why, so you're not paying for hardware you don't need.

03

Lock repairs

Sticky deadbolt, loose handle, latch that won't catch — we fix the lock instead of selling you a new one when the old one still has life. Customers tell us the price was reasonable with no hidden costs, and that's the deal every time.

04

Lock upgrades & security advice

Want a proper deadlock on the front door or a restricted key system for a share house? We'll talk through what suits the property, fit gear that meets Australian Standards, and keep it within a sensible budget.

05

After-hours callouts

Nights, weekends, public holidays — the phone gets answered and Joe gets in the van. No call centre, no waiting until Monday, no panic pricing because it's late.
