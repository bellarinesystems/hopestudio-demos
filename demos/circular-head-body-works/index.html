What we do

Services that cover the whole job

One shop, one phone call. From the first dent assessment to the finished respray, we handle it under the same roof on Smith Street.

// 01

Panel beating

Bent guards, creased doors, crumpled bonnets — we reshape the metal back to where it should be. It's the work locals specifically mention when they recommend us, and we take pride in getting the lines right.

// 02

Smash repairs

From minor rear-enders to bigger collision jobs, we'll assess the damage and walk you through what needs doing. We handle the panels, paint and mechanical bits so you've only got one shop to deal with.

// 03

Spray painting & colour matching

We match factory paint codes so the repaired panel blends in with the rest of the car. Whether it's a ute that's seen a few seasons or a near-new family wagon, the finish is the bit that puts a smile on your face.

// 04

Insurance claim repairs

We deal with the insurer's paperwork and quotes so you're not stuck on hold all afternoon. Drop the car off, we'll handle the back-and-forth and let you know when it's ready.

// 05

Quotes & assessments

Bring the car in and we'll have a proper look — no guessing over the phone. You'll get an honest read on what the damage is, what it'll cost and how long we'll need it.
