What Juan does

Lighting and small electrical, done with care

Most of our work is lighting upgrades for homeowners mid-renovation, but the small repair jobs and safety checks are just as welcome.

Downlight installation

Replacing tired old fittings or planning a fresh layout for a renovated room. We'll walk through spacing and switching with you before a single hole gets cut, so the finish matches what you actually pictured.

Pendant lighting

Feature pendants over kitchen islands, dining tables and stairwells, including the back-and-forth where you change your mind about the drop height. Like one of our customers put it, we're happy to make your ideas work even when those ideas keep shifting.

Switch & powerpoint upgrades

Swapping out yellowed switches, adding USB powerpoints, or tidying up that mess of double adaptors behind the telly. Neat work, matching plates, no painted-over screws left behind.

Exhaust fans & heated globes

Bathroom three-in-ones, ducted exhausts and heated globe replacements done to current Australian Standards. Good for foggy mirrors, mouldy ceilings and cold winter showers.

Safety checks & small repairs

Tripping circuits, dodgy fittings, RCDs that need updating — the small stuff that's been bugging you for months. We'll diagnose it, explain what's going on in plain English, and sort it on the spot where we can.
