What we do

Domestic, commercial, and the dodgy stuff in between

01

Home electrical work

Power points, light fittings, safety switches, switchboard upgrades and the dodgy bits the last owner left behind. We'll walk you through what needs doing and what can wait, the way we did for Marian when she had a list of old gear to replace.

02

Commercial fit-outs and maintenance

Shops, offices and small warehouses around the outer east — new fit-outs, repairs, exit lighting and ongoing maintenance. We work around your trading hours so the till keeps ringing.

03

Lighting design and installation

Downlights, LED upgrades, outdoor and security lighting for the back deck or the car park. We'll talk through where the light actually needs to fall before we start drilling holes.

04

Safety switches and switchboards

Old ceramic fuses, overloaded boards, missing RCDs — the stuff that quietly turns into a problem. We bring it all up to current Australian Standards and show you what changed and why.

05

24/7 emergency callouts

Power out, burning smell, board tripping and won't reset — ring us any hour and one of the team will get to you. Residential or commercial, Croydon and surrounds.
