Services

What Brett gets called out for

From urgent lockouts to careful upgrades, every job is handled the same way — turn up on time, give you a straight price, and finish properly before we leave.

Urgent rekeys

When keys go missing or a tenant moves out, we'll rekey your locks the same day so you can sleep easy. One Vermont family rang us in a panic and Brett rekeyed the house and walked them through their security setup before leaving.

Lockouts and emergency callouts

Locked out of the house at 10pm with the kids in the car? Ring Brett directly — no call centre, no dispatch fee surprise, just someone local who'll come and get you back inside.

Home lock upgrades

We fit deadlocks, restricted-key systems and proper door hardware that meets Australian Standards. Good for owners wanting better insurance peace of mind, or renters whose landlord has finally agreed to upgrade.

Letterbox and small jobs

Yes, we still do the little stuff — letterbox keys, broken padlocks, garden shed locks. No job is too small and we won't make you feel silly for asking about it.

Security advice for your home

While we're there fitting a lock, we'll have a proper look at your doors and windows and tell you honestly what's worth doing. No upsell, no scare tactics — just a Vermont local sharing what he'd do at his own place.
