What we do

Home locksmithing across the south-east

Most jobs are residential and most wrap inside a few hours. Here's the work that fills the diary.

01

Whole-house lock replacement

Every external door done in one visit, usually wrapped up within a few hours. Ideal if you've just moved in or you're not sure who else has a key.

02

Rekeying existing locks

We keep your current hardware and rekey it so old keys no longer work. A cheaper option when the locks themselves are still in good nick.

03

Lockouts & emergency call-outs

Locked out of the house or the key snapped in the door? Give us a ring and we'll get to you as quickly as we can, without the drama.

04

Deadlocks & security upgrades

Adding deadlocks, fitting stronger entry hardware, or sorting out a back door that's never felt quite right. We'll talk through what makes sense for your place before any work starts.
