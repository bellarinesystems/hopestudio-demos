What we do

Services on Murray Street

Everyday servicing and the kind of urgent help you need when something's gone sideways on the road.

01 · Emergency

Roadside rescue and emergency fit-ins

If you've hit something on the highway or your car's gone wonky on the run between Geelong and Warrnambool, ring us and we'll squeeze you in. One traveller hit an animal carcass and damaged their exhaust hanger — we got them sorted and back on the way to Melbourne the same day.

02 · Misfuel

Wrong fuel recovery

Put petrol in a diesel, or diesel in a petrol? Don't start the car — call us straight away and we'll come to you, drain the tank, and get you moving again.

03 · Diagnostics

Honest diagnostics and inspections

Bring it in if something doesn't sound right and we'll have a proper look before quoting any work. If the noise turns out to be nothing — like tyre roar mistaken for a bearing — we'll tell you straight and you won't get a bill for the look-over.

04 · Servicing

Logbook servicing and general repairs

Routine servicing, brakes, suspension, exhaust work and the rest of the everyday stuff that keeps a car healthy. We work to Australian Standards and we'll explain what we found in plain English.

05 · Travellers

Help for travellers passing through Colac

If you're heading along the Great Ocean Road or driving back to the city and something's gone wrong, we know what it's like to be a long way from home. Drop in at 324 Murray Street and we'll do our best to fit you in that day.

Questions

Things we get asked

Ring us as soon as you can and we'll do our best. We've helped plenty of travellers heading back to Melbourne or down the Great Ocean Road, and we know being stranded out of town is no fun. We can't always promise same-day, but we'll be straight with you about timing.

Don't start the engine — that's the main thing. Give us a call straight away and we'll talk you through it, and if you're nearby we can come out and drain the tank for you. The sooner you ring, the less damage there'll be.

If you come in worried about something and it turns out to be nothing, we don't charge for the look. Proper diagnostic work on a real fault is a different matter and we'll quote that up front. Either way, you'll know before we start.

We're at 324 Murray Street, Colac — easy to find on the main run through town. Best bet is to ring (03) 5208 9033 before you head over so we know to expect you.
