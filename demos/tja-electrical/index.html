What we do

Honest sparky work for outer-east homes.

01

Split system power & wiring

We run the dedicated circuit and install the power point your new split system needs, then coordinate cleanly with your aircon installer. Ideal for homeowners getting heating and cooling sorted before summer or winter hits.

02

Power points & GPO upgrades

Whether you need an extra outlet behind the telly, a double in the kitchen, or outdoor weatherproof points for the BBQ area, we'll add them tidily without tearing the place apart. Great for older Mooroolbark homes that were built with two power points per room.

03

Lighting installs & swaps

From swapping tired downlights for LEDs through to fresh pendants over the kitchen island and sensor lights down the side of the house. We talk you through what'll actually look good and what'll save you on the power bill.

04

Safety switches & switchboards

We upgrade old ceramic-fuse boards to modern switchboards with proper RCDs, so the whole house is protected to current Australian Standards. Recommended for any home that hasn't had the board touched in 20+ years.

05

Urgent call-outs & small jobs

Tripping circuit, dead power point, light that won't stop flickering — ring us and we'll do our best to squeeze you in, the same way we have for plenty of other locals. No job too small and no drama about it.
