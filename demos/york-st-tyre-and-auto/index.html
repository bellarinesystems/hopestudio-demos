What we do

Tyres, servicing & the
jobs that go past tyres.

01

New tyres, fitted & balanced

Pick a set that suits the car and how you drive, then we fit, balance and align so it actually handles right. Customers tell us they're stunned at the difference once the new rubber's on.

02

Emergency breakdown repairs

If you've been towed in or limped in, ring us and we'll get you in the queue that day. We've had people in with dead fuel pumps on a Tuesday and back on the road by Wednesday.

03

Logbook servicing

Full logbook services that keep your new-car warranty intact, done to manufacturer schedules. We use the right oils and filters and we tell you straight what needs doing now versus what can wait.

04

Mechanical diagnostics & repairs

Strange noise, warning light, won't start — we'll work out what's actually going on instead of throwing parts at it. Suits drivers who've been mucked around elsewhere and just want a straight answer.

05

Wheel alignment & balancing

Proper four-wheel alignment on the gear, so your tyres last longer and the car tracks straight. Worth doing any time you put new tyres on or after you've hit a serious pothole.
