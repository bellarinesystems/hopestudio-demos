What we do

Cut, fitted, programmed, installed.

01

Lockouts & emergency call-outs

Keys snapped in the door, kids inside, or the office shut you out on a Monday morning — give us a ring and we'll get someone moving. We cover Orange and the surrounding rural blocks, so you're not waiting on a tech from Bathurst.

02

Broken handles & door hardware

Back-door handles cop a beating, and when they go we'll come out, assess what's there, and replace it without tearing the whole door apart. It's the bread-and-butter job folks like Tony have thanked us for — quick visit, fixed, done.

03

Car keys & transponder programming

Lost the only key to the ute or need a spare cut and coded? We program transponder keys and remotes for most makes on the road around here, so you're not towing it to a dealer two hours away.

04

Home & business security upgrades

Rekeying after a tenant changes over, restricted key systems for the shopfront, deadlocks that actually meet the Australian Standard. We'll walk the property with you and sort out what's worth doing and what isn't.

05

CCTV & alarm installs

Full camera setups for the farm shed, the warehouse, or the front of the house — supplied, installed, and shown to you properly before we leave. We stick around for the questions, not just the invoice.
